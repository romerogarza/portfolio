About

My journey into Data Engineering started over 7 years ago. After graduating with my Master's in Information Systems with a concentration in Business Analytics, I began my career at Cerner Corporation as a Reporting Analyst and quickly moved up to a Business Intelligence Developer, taking on more data responsibilities. I've worked on the Research & Development (R&D) team within Cerner to push innovation using data from the lowest levels of data engineering to telling the story to clients and showcasing our findings, and most importantly, how data can save lives in the Healthcare Industry.

I've then moved on from Cerner and focused more on the public sector, working at the Not-for-Profit large Hospital system within the North Kansas City Area. In this role as a Data Engineer I've been able to focus on my skills entirely, from the beginning of the projects of fact finding, working with individual departments to understand their requirements and needs, and more so to understand the story they want to tell. From here I dive back into what I know and engineer data to automate what they need and build the visual. This role has helped me take lead on projects and manage everything from start to finish.

Services

Every business wants to make good decisions. And good decisions rely on good information. But how you communicate that information matters. The ETL Process

Get more from your data by consolidating your reports into one place. I can do this through my ETL process, which stands for “extract, transform, load,” to move data from one database, multiple databases, or other sources to a unified repository—typically a data warehouse. It enables data analysis to provide actionable business information, effectively preparing data for analysis and your business intelligence processes. Check out the Enterprise Data Warehouse I recently created.

Data Engineering

Once Data is consolidated, I can write queries that aggregate just the way you need it, in order to tell the right story. Each part of the dashboard tells a different story, and thus needs a different aggregatation. I can do this for all your needs with automation. By using this method, it allows the refresh process to run smoothly, simply load the data and refresh.

Data Analytics

Use your Data to empower your decisions. Data analytics is the science of analyzing raw data to make conclusions about that information. Data analytics can help your business optimize its performance, perform more efficiently, maximize profit, or make more strategically-guided decisions.

Data Automation

With my methods of extracting and automating SQL jobs to take your data and load it into your databases on a schedule, it allows for your dashboards to refresh daily or even hourly. Get the most from your data by getting results as fast as possible.

Data Visualization

Data visualization is the graphical representation of information and data. By using visual elements like charts, graphs, and maps, my data visualization techniques provide an accessible way to see and understand trends, outliers, and patterns in data. Additionally, it provides an excellent way for your business to present data to non-technical audiences without confusion.

Telling the Story

This is the most important piece of Data Analysis. Data storytelling is the ability to effectively communicate insights from a dataset using narratives and visualizations. It can be used to put data insights into context for and inspire action from your audience. By having a story in place, more users will visit your dashboards frequently, giving more opportunities to succeed with data.
